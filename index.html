Nimish P. Hathi

Hi! I am a Support/STScI Scientist at Space Telescope Science Institute (STScI) in Baltimore, MD, USA. My main research interest is studying distant galaxies to understand how they form and evolve with time to become Milky Way and other present-day galaxies. I got my PhD in Physics/Astronomy from Arizona State University (USA) in August 2008. I have worked at University of California - Riverside (USA), Carnegie Observatories (USA), and Laboratoire d'Astrophysique de Marseille (France) as a Postdoctoral Researcher.

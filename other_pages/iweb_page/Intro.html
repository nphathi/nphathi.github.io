Hi! I am currently working as a Postdoctoral Research Associate at Laboratoire d’Astrophysique de Marseille in Marseille, France. I got my PhD from Arizona State University in August 2008. I have worked at University of California, Riverside, CA and Carnegie Observatories, Pasadena, CA as a Postdoc. I study distant galaxies to understand how they form and evolve with time to become Milky Way and other present-day galaxies.

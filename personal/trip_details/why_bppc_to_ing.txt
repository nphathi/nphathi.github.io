# Advantages of ING bank (France) over BPPC

(1) NPH-PNH joint account --- BPPC only gave single NPH account

(2) Both got free mastercards   --- BPPC cards had fees

(3) Both cards have larger limits 800 Euros/week --- BPPC had 300-600 Euros/week 

(4) We can use any ATM for free to withdraw money --- non-BPPC ATMs charged fees 

(5) ING website shows all transactions free --- BPPC showed only last 5 transactions free

(6) ING money transfer within Europe is free --- BPPC charged fees

(7) ING gave a cheque book --- BPPC did not give any cheque book

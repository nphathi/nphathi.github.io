# Trip details (NY-New York, RI-Rhode Island, NH-New Hampshire)

3rd October
-- Left home at 1.30pm, after 1 stop and some traffic reached NY at 5.30pm.
-- Parking at 200 Park Avenue (Metlife Building).
-- Visited Summit One Vanderbilt for about 2 hours (5.30-7.30pm). 
-- Mirrored floors. Nice city and sunset views.
-- Spent some time at Grand Central station.
-- Dinner at Taco Bell in Hoboken-NJ. 
-- Hotel stay at Holiday Inn (Hoboken) on 3rd night.

4th October
-- Hirav picked up NPV-PHV from JFK airport (came from India on Air India) at 8am.
-- It was about an hour drive to JFK from Hoboken, NJ.
-- HPV-NPV-PHV ame to our hotel at about 10.15am. Took some time to freshen up.
-- At 11am we all left by 2 cars. Mazda parked at Hirav's friend's place in Newark.
-- Around noon, we all left NJ for RI in a rental car (Chevy Suburban Van).
-- Reached Providence-RI after a couple of stops (Ice cream, Dunkin, Gas station) and traffic at about 5pm.
-- Stay at SpringHill Suites Providence West Warwick hotel (Marriott) for 2 nights.
-- Around 6pm, walked on the Providence River Pedestrian Bridge for city views. 
-- Dinner at 'India' Indian restaurant after some wait.
-- Back to hotel after the dinner (around 10.30pm).

5th October
-- Breakfast in hotel, left at 10.45am for White Mountains-NH (normally ~3.5 hours drive).
-- After 1 stop and a lot of traffic, reached the Hancock overlook at Kancamagus Hwy-NH at 4pm.
-- Nice views of fall foliage. Lot of people as this is the peak time (and weekend).
-- NPH drove Suburban from overlook to Loon mountain gondola ride (~10 miles) -- while Hirav took pictures.
-- Loon mountain gondola ride at 5pm, nice views from the ropeway, cold at the top.
-- Evening 'snacks' around 6.30pm at Subway, dinner at Honest Restaurant near Boston at 9.30pm.
-- Reached back to the hotel at about 11.15pm.

6th October
-- Breakfast in hotel, left at 10.30am for Newport-RI (~30 min drive).
-- Mansions tour (drive), Cliff walk, fudge shop and lunch at Pasta Beach restaurant (~12pm).
-- Left Newport for LaGuardia airport-NY (normally about 3 hrs drive) at 1pm. 
-- After 1 stop and lot of traffic, reached at 6pm. Dropped off HPV-NPV-PHV for their flight to Raleigh at 8.15pm.
-- N-P-H drove to Newark to pick up Mazda and return the rental car at Newark ariport (~8pm).
-- Dinner at Taco Bell, left NJ at 9.20pm, reached home at 12.20am (no stops, some traffic).

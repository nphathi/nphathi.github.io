"Karmanyevadhikaraste, Maa Phaleshu Kadaachana,
Maa Karmaphalaheturbhooh, Maa Te Sangostvakarmani"
 (Gita-2,47)

....... meaning .......

You have but the right to perform action; you have no hold on the results thereof.
May you not seek the rewards of action and may you never engage in wrong action.
